BowlingJS

London based meetup for developers

BowlingJS is an informal group of developers who like to meet up and throw bowling balls at pins whilst discussing code, hipster tech and community. Likely discussion topics are: functional programming, compile to JavaScript languages, es6, promises and iojs...alongside more important things like where to get the best coffee.

Who can come?

Anyone! Everyone! We're not fussy. We'll probably talk about tech and code so it'll help if you're interested in that sort of thing, but aside from that everyone is welcome.

How does it work?

Grab a ticket when the meetup is announced. We'll book lanes at the alley and all you need to do is turn up and pay for your bowling. If you don't get a ticket or if you arrive after 7pm (when we start the bowling itself) we can't guarantee there'll be space on the lane.
